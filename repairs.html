Repairs & Custom Djembes

Bringing life to your djembe

Repairs

If your skin has popped or you just want a new round of fancy rope put on your djembe, look no further than The Drum Hut. I'll repair and refinish your djembe in style!

Make yourself a quote by following the guide below and if you're not sure exactly what you're after, don't hesitate to get in touch and we can talk about the options.

Custom Djembes

Everything that's available for djembe repairs is also avilable as a custom option for any of The Drum Hut's djembes shells. It'll all cost the same too. The prices quoted on the djembes page are for djembes skinned with goat skin, standard rope and finished with a clean cut finish. The cost for the other finishes are given below and High Quality Rope will add an extra £1 per crown knot (typically £30). Calf skin will add £15 and deer skin will add £25.

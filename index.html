The Drum Hut is owned and run by me, Matthew Crisp, from a small office near Edinburgh city center. I started tinkering with djembes a few years ago, when my first djembe skin popped. After a few friends asked me to repair their djembes, I decided to pursue the hobby started buying broken djembes on ebay to fix and sell. Now, a couple of years down the line, I have found a supplier of truly magnificent djembe shells from Guinea and The Drum Hut has become my attempt to make a living from the instruments that I've loved for so many years.

Edinburgh is The Drum Hut's home, but I ship all of my products across the UK and EU. The Premium djembes I stock are from Conakry in Guinea and are carved by the exceptionally talented Alseny Camara. Each djembe is a work of art unto itself. They're skinned here in Scotland using the highest quality skins from Mali and Guinea and racing-spec yacht rope, meaning that these are amongst highest quality instruments of their type on the planet.

I still regularly find djembes in need of love and attention and these can be found in the Beginner and Intermediate Djembes section of the Djembes page. I try to keep a stock for all budgets whenever possible.

The Pascal

An aggressive sounding djembe with a loud, resonant voice and uncomprimising carving to match. Perfect for those who want to be both seen and heard. Named after 17th Century mathematician Blaise Pascal and the famous "Pascal's Triangle" - a triangular array that can be seen in the carving on the foot of this djembe.

11" Ghanaian Tweneboa Djembe

A small and pretty djembe that weighs very little. With it's sharp sound and surprisingly large bass, this makes a perfect drum for the djembefola who wants good sound at a very low price. Your back will thank you for choosing this lightweight djembe!

An unfortunate accident with a pair of scissors whilst skinning this djembe resulted in the need for two lengths of rope to be used, so the final few Verticals are roped with striped white rope.
